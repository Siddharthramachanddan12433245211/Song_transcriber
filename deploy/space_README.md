# Shabd — free song & speech transcription

Upload your recording — a cover, practice take, demo, voice memo, lecture or
podcast — and get the words back as lyrics text (.txt) or subtitles
(.srt/.vtt). Powered by faster-whisper on the free CPU tier.

- Files are processed one at a time and deleted right after processing.
- Nothing is stored, shared, or used for training.
- Please only upload recordings you have the rights to.
